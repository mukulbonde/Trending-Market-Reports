Emerging trends in Resin Applications
Advanced epoxy resins: The development of epoxy resins with thermal stability and electrical properties improved is reshaping the market. These resins are particularly crucial in applications that require high reliability, such as electric vehicles (EV).
Focus on sustainability: The industry is shifting towards bio-based resins to reduce the environmental impact. Companies are currently exploring renewable raw materials to create sustainable insulation solutions.
Smart Technologies: The integration of smart technologies into insulation materials is gaining ground. Intelligent monitoring systems that can detect insulation failures before they occur are becoming essential in critical applications.
