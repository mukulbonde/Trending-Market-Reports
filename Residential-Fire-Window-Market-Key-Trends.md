Recent Innovations in the Residential Fire Window Market: What You Need to Know
The residential fire window market has seen significant advancements recently, addressing safety issues and improving design. With an increasing emphasis on fire safety in residential buildings, demand for specialized windows is on the rise.

Emerging technologies in fire safety
Innovations such as intumescent materials, which expand when exposed to heat, are becoming standard. These materials help create a barrier that prevents flames from spreading during a fire. Research indicates that homes equipped with fire-resistant windows can reduce heat transfer by up to 75%.

Regulatory and Standard Changes
Recent changes to building codes in several regions are prompting homeowners and builders to prioritize fire-resistant windows. For example, the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA) has updated guidelines requiring enhanced fire safety features in new residential construction. These changes have led to a growing market for fire-resistant windows, with some manufacturers reporting a 30% increase in sales since the guidelines were implemented.
