Technological innovations driving growth
Recent innovations in solar technology are a key driver of this market boom. High-efficiency solar panels, such as monocrystalline options, have improved energy production and reduced space requirements. In particular, bifacial solar panels, which capture sunlight from both sides, are gaining ground, allowing homeowners to maximize energy generation.

Future Outlook
